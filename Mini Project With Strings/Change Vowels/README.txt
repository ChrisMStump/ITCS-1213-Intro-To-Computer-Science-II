PROJECT TITLE: Change Vowels
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: Use the replace method within the String library.
VERSION or DATE: 2-25-2015

The program takes in the following text from the apples.txt:

I like to eat, I like to eat, eat apples and bananas.

Then it uses the replace method to change the vowels as follows:

E leke te eet, E leke te eet, eet epples end benenes.
A laka ta aat, A laka ta aat, aat applas and bananas.
I liki ti iit, I liki ti iit, iit ipplis ind bininis.
O loko to oot, O loko to oot, oot opplos ond bononos.
U luku tu uut, U luku tu uut, uut upplus und bununus.
